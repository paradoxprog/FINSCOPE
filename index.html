Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At FINSCOPE, we prioritize your financial privacy. This policy outlines how your "Financial Twin" data is handled.

1. Data Encryption

All financial inputs (income, expenses, goals) are encrypted during transit and stored securely using Firebase. We utilize session-based encryption for your AI simulations.

2. AI Processing

This data is used solely to provide your personalized advice and is not used to train global models without your explicit consent.

3. No Third-Party Selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information to third-party advertisers or data brokers.

4. Local Storage

Certain preference data is stored locally on your device to ensure a fast and seamless dashboard experience.
